#### 求和的积分近似

$f(x)$是单调递减的连续函数，则

$$\int_{m}^{n+1} f(x) \mathrm{d} x \leq \sum_{j=m}^{n} f(j) \leq \int_{m-1}^{n} f(x) \mathrm{d} x$$

$f(x)$是单调递增的连续函数，则

$$\int_{m-1}^{n} f(x) \mathrm{d} x \leq \sum_{j=m}^{n} f(j) \leq \int_{m}^{n+1} f(x) \mathrm{d} x$$
